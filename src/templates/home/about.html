About CSai

CSai is a web application that allows users to ask questions to an "AI tutor" and receive answers in a conversational manner. CSai is written in Python, built using the Flask framework, and leverages the power of the OpenAI API. The application is currently in beta and is not available to the public.

"Breakthroughs are increasingly improving what A.I. is capable of—opening new and exciting avenues of education."

As a computer science student, it can be challenging to quickly get help when stuck on a problem or programming concept. Emailing a professor can be time-consuming if they are slow to respond. Posting to the online forums can be hit-or-miss, and often results in a lot of noise or misinformation. Asking for help from friends or classmates is certainly a possibility... IF you are lucky enough to know someone with the right knowledge.

Without a doubt, finding help with programming concepts is challenging. And for self-taught individuals, it can be even harder. These are the thoughts that inspired the creation of CSai. A tool that can help students and self-taught individuals quickly get answers to their programming questions. Imagine a future where we can realiably depend on A.I. to help us learn and grow. CSai is a small step in that direction.

As technology continues to become more ingrained into our daily lives, Computer Science educators are only going to become more sought-after. Fortunately, breakthroughs are increasingly improving what A.I. is capable of—opening new and exciting avenues of education.
